Smart Album Sync: hands-free photo import

Most progress photo apps require you to manually import photos every time you take one. GainFrame's Smart Album Sync eliminates that step entirely.

  1. Select a source album. Pick any album from your Photos library ‚Äî for example, a "Gym" album you already organize your selfies into.
  2. GainFrame watches it. Whenever new photos appear in that album, GainFrame automatically imports and processes them ‚Äî AI classification, scoring, and timeline insertion happen in the background.

  4. Deletion blocklist. If you delete a photo from GainFrame, the sync engine remembers it and won't re-import it ‚Äî even if it's still in the source album.
